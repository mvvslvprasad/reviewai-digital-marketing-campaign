# Review AI Tech & Solution — Digital Marketing Campaign

## Overview

This project presents a complete 6-month digital marketing campaign strategy for **Review AI Tech & Solution**, an AI-powered reputation management platform designed for freelancers, startups, and SMB agencies.

The campaign focuses on building brand awareness, generating qualified leads, increasing paid conversions, and establishing a strong online community using a multi-channel digital marketing approach.

Developed as part of the **CodeAlpha Internship — Task 3**.



## Campaign Objectives

* Achieve **5M+ impressions** across digital channels
* Generate **15,000+ qualified leads**
* Reach **12% free-to-paid conversion rate**
* Build a **50K+ engaged freelancer/startup community**



## Target Audience

* Freelancers using platforms like Upwork, Fiverr, and Toptal
* Early-stage startups
* SMB agency owners
* Student entrepreneurs and Gen Z founders



## Marketing Channels Used

* Google Search Ads
* LinkedIn Sponsored Ads
* Instagram & Meta Ads
* YouTube Marketing
* Influencer Marketing
* SEO & Content Marketing
* Retargeting Campaigns
